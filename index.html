FAQ

What is ThetaHacks?

ThetaHacks is a 24 hour event where students from all over the Bay Area can come together to make something together, whether software or hardware!

Who can participate?

Anyone in high school can join! If you've graduated already, consider becoming a mentor! If you are in middle school, email us for details.

How do I form a team? Do I need a team?

You may form a team with up to 4 members! If you would like to go solo, thats perfectly fine too.

Does this cost money?

Nope. ThetaHacks is free, and always will be! Everything from food to swag is covered for all participants.

What if I cant code?

If you can't code, thats totally not a problem! We will be hosting workshops so you can learn, so please join us.

What should I bring?

Bring your school student ID (or a government issued ID), laptop, charger, water bottle, and waiver (details soon). If you want to sleep (yolo-ing is overrated), feel free to bring a sleeping bag and toiletries.

What are the prizes? Rules? Code of Conduct?

We will be posting a DevPost link soon with all the technical details. We plan to have some pretty epic prizes, so bring your best ideas!
